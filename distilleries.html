Top Distilleries in Scotland

Glenfiddich

Location: Dufftown, Speyside

History: Founded in 1887, it remains family-owned and offers tours of its production process.

Macallan

Location: Craigellachie, Speyside

History: Established in 1824, it's famous for its sherry cask maturation.

Lagavulin

Location: Islay

History: Operating since 1816, it's known for its peaty, smoky whisky.

Laphroaig

Location: Islay

History: Founded in 1815, it offers the "Friends of Laphroaig" program.

Talisker

Location: Isle of Skye

History: Established in 1830, it's known for its maritime character.

Highland Park

Location: Orkney

History: Founded in 1798, it uses traditional peat drying methods.
